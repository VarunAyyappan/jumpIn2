Character Movement:
Use the A key on the keyboard to move your character left.
Use the D key to move your character right.
Use the W key to have your character jump.

Pause Menu:
Use p on the keyboard to enter into the pause menu anytime during the game
The pause menu will let you reenter the game, look up these directions, and quit the game.

Parabloa Problem Input:
Input your answer to the question in the input given near the bottom of the screen.
You get three trys to answer the question.
You will get hints when you get the answer wrong.

How to Solve the Problems:
1) First Stage Problems:
    y = a(x-h)^2 + k
    h = vertex x coordinate, k = vertex y coordinate
    Then solve for a by plugging in a non-vertx point.

2) Second Stage Problems:
    Same as first stage problems.

3) Third Stage Problems:
    Use first stage steps, then open up parenthesis and simplify .

4) Challange Problems:
    Use a system of equations to sole theses.
